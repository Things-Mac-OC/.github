## What is Things for Mac?

Things is a powerful and elegant task management application designed specifically for macOS. It helps you organize your tasks, projects, and goals in a clean and intuitive interface. With support for areas, projects, deadlines, and repeating tasks, Things provides a flexible system that adapts to your workflow.

The app integrates seamlessly with the macOS ecosystem, including support for Dark Mode, Touch Bar, and keyboard shortcuts. Whether you're planning your day, managing complex projects, or simply keeping track of personal errands, Things offers a thoughtful and efficient way to stay productive.
